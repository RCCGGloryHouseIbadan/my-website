FROM OUR PASTOR

Welcome To Our Church

Welcome to the official website of RCCG Glory House - Covenant Sanctuary, Region 21 Headquarters Church! We are delighted to have you here as a member, visitor, or someone seeking spiritual guidance. Our church, under the esteemed leadership of Pastor Ezekiel Afolayan as the Pastor in Charge (PICR) of Region 21, is committed to providing a nurturing environment for spiritual growth and a vibrant community where everyone can experience God's love and grace.

RCCG Glory House is a dynamic and thriving community of believers located in the heart of Oyo State, Nigeria. As part of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG), one of the largest and fastest-growing Pentecostal churches in the world, we are dedicated to fulfilling the Great Commission by spreading the gospel, making disciples, and impacting lives with the transformative power of Jesus Christ.

Our Children Church is a whole lot of wonderful experience for the various age groups. Our teenagers have their fellowships in an atmosphere that grows them in all spheres of life. In our adult Church, the Word of God works wonders as the whole gospel is preached with God bearing witness to His Word daily. As a community impact church, we carry out regular medical outreach, corporate social responsibility projects, and other support outreaches to reflect the reality of the love of Jesus to all.

We have service platforms for every member to use their gifts and talents to serve God and contribute their quota to the furtherance of the work of God's Kingdom on earth. We will be glad to have you join us.

“As you go through this site, you will get to know more about this Church and discover your place in it. God has brought you here for a royal purpose and we know that He will see through its fulfillment.”

OUR VISION & MISSION

To spread the Word of God to the ends of the earth.

The Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) has the vision to spread the Word of God to the ends of the earth. It is a Bible-believing church, where the teaching and a missionary approach emphasize the proper conduct and moral behaviour of people. The church has an appeal to all types of people worldwide, with a determination to teach people in their various geographical areas, stages of secular life and levels of spiritual development.

Mission&Vision

  1. To make heaven.
  2. To take as many people with us.
  3. To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.
  4. To accomplish No. 1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle.
  5. To accomplish No. 2 and 3 above, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.
  6. We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ.
WHO WE ARE

Our Team & Leadership

Here at Glory House, we believe in unity, teamwork, and inspiring the leader in one another. Our team is committed to using our God-given gifts and experiences to their fullest in order to create an atmosphere where people can come to know God, and grow spiritually and physically. Apart from the few ministers displayed on this page, we also have a group of outstanding church workers who serve in several unique capacities; they are the life of our church.

Pastor & Pastor (Mrs.) Ezekiel Afolayan

Pastor-In-Charge of Region 21 (PICR 21)

Pastor Ezekiel Afolayan is a Pastor in The Redeemed Christian Church of God. He holds an A-level certificate from Kwara State Polytechnic, a B.Sc. from University of Ilorin, a MSc. in Geography from University of Ilorin, Kwara State, and a PhD from the Redeemed Christian Bible College. He was ordained a full Pastor in the Redeemed Christian Church of God in 1996.

He has authored many Christian books, presented many papers and attended conferences both at local and international levels, some of his books are includes but not limited to Master of the Deep, Prayer Missile for hard problems, Finishing well as well as Divine Favour. Pastor Afolayan served as Hostel representative of Fellowship of Christian Students (FCS), Kwara Polytechnic. With a strong passion for the liberation of souls, God has used him to touch many lives in the area of deliverance and evangelism. He has served in several capacities including:

  • Assistant Continental Overseer Africa Continent 3 (SATCO) - August 2023 till date.
  • PIC Region 21 - August 2022 till date.
  • Pioneer Regional Pastor, RCCG Region 29, Cross River State.
  • Regional Pastor, RCCG Region 4, Kwara State.
  • Pioneer Regional Pastor, RCCG Region 18(Sokoto, Kebbi and Zamfara).
  • Provincial Pastor, RCCG Lagos Province 10, Agege-Lagos.
  • Provincial Pastor, RCCG North 13, Nasarawa State.
  • Area Pastor, RCCG Green Pastures, Abuja
  • Assistant State Sunday School Coordinator, North 1.
  • Area Pastor, RCCG Chapel of Life, Abuja.

Pastor Ezekiel is married to Pastor (Mrs) Ajoke Afolayan in April, 1991 and they are blessed with four Children to the glory of God. Pastor (Mrs) Ajoke Afolayan obtained B.Sc. in Mathematics(Education) from the University of Ilorin. She currently works with Federal Ministry of Education (FEQAS), Ibadan as Deputy Director.

F. A. Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What time is your Sunday Service?

We hold a single worship service each Sunday in our church auditorium. We start with a Sunday school by 8:00 am followed by a celebration service by 9:00 am. We also hold a monthly thanksgiving service on every first Sunday.

Do I have to belong to your denomination to attend Jesus House?

Not at all! There are many in our congregation that come from a church background other than the RCCG. We are a “patchwork” church - people with different church backgrounds “woven together” by God's grace.

What kind of things do you do in your worship service?

We strive to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere at Glory House. We greet each other so everyone feels welcomed; we share announcements and church news; we sing, dance, and listen to our choir perform beautiful music; we lift the congregation's joys and concerns to God through thanksgiving and prayer; we read the Bible and talk about it through the sermon; and we collect an offering to further God's work.

Do you have a parking space in your church?

Yes we do. We have a big parking space around the church premises where your car can be safely parked. We have Redeemed Volunteers who together with security agencies, will watch over your car while you worship.

Do you hold Online Services?

Yes, we hold online services. If you are unable to make it to church for any reason, you can join our live services on our Watch Us Live page. We make video broadcasts of our Sunday worship services. At this time, we also hold Bible Studies online. Kindly check our events calendar for the schedules of such meetings.

How do I get more Involved in the Church?

A perfect place to start is by visiting a small group, which provides an excellent opportunity to get to know more people and grow. Our house fellowships and natural groups are a place to start. You can also take your involvement a step further by joining the church workforce.

What do you offer for Children on Sunday?

We offer Sunday school and impact classes for children aged 3 years through to 12 years. We also cater to infants, crawlers, walkers, and newborns up to the age of 2 during each of our worship services. Our teachers and minders teach Jesus-centered topics in a fun and engaging way in our secure children's area which allows you to concentrate during services.
