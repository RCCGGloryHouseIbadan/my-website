WELCOME TO OUR CHURCH

Dear Beloved,

Greetings in the name of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ. By this medium, I want to appreciate you for visiting our website, glory be to God who has made it possible. I'm pleased to inform you that Glory House(Covenant Sanctuary), the Regional Headquarter for Region 21, Ibadan, of The Redeemed Christian Church of God has a warm and receptive atmosphere conducive for your spiritual growth, physical well-being, and prosperity. The Church Auditorium is located opposite Zartech Industries Headquarters, Oluyole Estate, Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria.

I encourage you to take advantage of our Church services like the House fellowship meetings that hold every Sunday evening to integrate yourself fully into our Church family. Kindly check the centre closest to you here.

Furthermore, we have Believers' and Baptismal classes to start you off on your Christian journey. Our workers-in-training program is designed to equip you for service in the vineyard, while our branch of The Redeemed Christian Church of God Bible College, School of Ministries and School of Disciples will deepen your knowledge and relationship with God.

Additionally, we have various groups in the Church that can afford you the opportunity to do service to your maker and also relate with men and women of like minds. I must also add that we have periodic programs designed to enrich your life. Be assured that whatever level you are, we are prepared to elevate your relationship with God. Please feel free to contact us for assistance in this regard.

Kindly make Glory House your permanent place of worship. I trust God to work wonders in and with your life.

-Pastor Yomi Dada

Coordinating Pastor
