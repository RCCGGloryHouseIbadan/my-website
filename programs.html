Get to know our run our Services
Sunday Service

Celebration Service, 8:00am - 11:00am

Thanksgiving Service, 8:00am - 12noon

At our Sunday Services, you will experience a sense of joy and gratitude. We aim to provide a complete and fulfilling experience, where all aspects of our gathering are carefully designed to bring clarity and meaning to your spiritual journey

Prayer Warriors' Vigil

Every Monday, 10:00 PM - 2:00 AM

Join us every Monday for our Prayer Warriors' Vigil, where we come together to seek divine guidance and blessings. It's a time for deep reflection and spiritual connection.

Digging Deep (Bible Study)

Every Tuesday, 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM

Join us for our weekly Bible Study session, known as "Digging Deep," every Tuesday from 5:00 PM to 6:30 PM. It's an opportunity to explore and delve deep into the teachings of the Bible, gaining a better understanding of its wisdom and guidance.

Victory At Last

Every Wednesday, 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Join us every Wednesday for "Victory At Last." This is a special gathering from 9:00 AM to 11:00 AM where we come together to pray towards our victories, seek inspiration, and share in the joy of achieving success in our spiritual journeys. All are welcome!

Faith Clinic (Prayer Meeting)

Every Thursday, 5:30 PM - 6:30 PM

Join us for our weekly "Faith Clinic" prayer meeting every Thursday from 5:30 PM to 6:30 PM. It's a time for spiritual growth, deepening your faith, and seeking divine guidance through prayer. All are welcome to attend.

Mount Zion Deliverance Service (A Regional Program)

Every 3rd Saturday of the Month, 7:00 AM - 10:00 AM

Join us for our monthly "Mount Zion Deliverance Service," a regional program that occurs every 3rd Saturday of the month from 7:00 AM to 10:00 AM. It's a time of spiritual renewal, deliverance, and inspiration. All are welcome to participate in this enriching experience.

Red Letter Day (Vigil) - A Regional Program

Every 3rd Friday in July, 9:00 PM - 3:00 AM

Join us for our special "Red Letter Day Vigil," a regional program held every 3rd Friday in July from 9:00 PM to 3:00 AM. This event is a time of spiritual reflection, prayer, and celebration. All are invited to participate in this significant and enriching experience.

Beginning The Month With Jesus

Every First Day of the Month, 6:00am - 7:00am

Join us to Begin The Month With Jesus on the first day of each month. This spiritual gathering runs from 6:00 AM to 7:00 AM and is a wonderful way to start the month with prayer, and inspiration.

Anointing Service

Every Last Sunday of the Month, 8:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Join us for our monthly "Anointing Service" held every last Sunday of the month from 8:00 AM to 11:30 AM. This service is dedicated to seeking blessings and divine anointing. It's a time for spiritual renewal and inspiration. All are welcome to attend and experience the anointing.

Holy Communion Service

Every First Sunday of the Month, 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Join us for our "Holy Communion Service" held every first Sunday of the month from 5:00 PM to 6:00 PM. This sacred service is an opportunity to partake in the Holy Communion, reflect on our faith, and draw closer to the teachings of Jesus. All are welcome to participate in this spiritually enriching experience.

Let's Go A Fishing (Evangelism)

Occurs Every April and December

This is a program, which takes place every April and December. This is an opportunity to actively engage in spreading the teachings of faith and reaching out to others with the message of love and salvation. All are encouraged to participate in this meaningful outreach.
