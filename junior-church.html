Glory House Junior Church

Raising a Generation of Godly and Outstanding Children
ABOUT US

Who We Are.

The Junior Church of the Redeemed Christian Church of God, Glory House, Covenant Sanctuary, Region 21 Headquarters is a worship centre where teens and children are groomed and taught the way of the Lord. The Bible says in Proverbs 22:6 "Train up a child in the way he should go, and when he is old, he will not depart from it". In accordance with the vision of the Mission through our mother-in-Israel, Pastor (Mrs.) Folu Adeboye, (Mummy G. O.), our children are to be taught the way of the Lord.

Also, it is a place to belong, grow, discover talents and help the teens and children to become who God has created them to be. We also train our teens and children morals and every thing necessary for them to navigate through the life and come out great, according to I Peter 2: 9

What We Do.

We have online bible study for our teenagers weekly on the WhatsApp platform of Regional Teens Excos, we hold our Sunday services starting with Sunday School by 8:00am every Sunday which dovetail into the main glorious Sunday Service between 9:00am and 11:00am. We participate (virtually) every third Saturday of the month in the Teens Global Summit, organised by the Directorate of Teens and Children Education (DTCE), a program specially designed for teenagers to address issues that concerns their lives and well being.

Also, we have our LILLY AMONG THORNS every February, this is a program organised to celebrate our teenagers February 14 - instead for them to be deceived by the worldly Valentine Day. We organise special program for our children every May 27, to mark the children day.

We organise Regional Holy Ghost service for our teenagers every May 1, being a public holiday at the Regional level, while we organise the same program at Province level every October 1. We organise National Young Leaders Retreat for our teens excos every November at the Regional Headquarters, this is a program aimed at empowering our teens excos to be great leaders in every spheres of life.

Camping/Camp out:. We organise special program as we camp out teens and children during the yearly Easter and December festive periods. We plan to have our Christmas Carol every December, aside from various activities to celebrate the birth of our Lord Jesus Christ during Christmas break.

Generally, our teens and children Department is a place every teens and children and teachers will always want to be because we have conducive environment for learning, growth and development both in Christ and life generally. Our Coordinator and teachers are so friendly and children loving.

As teachers we keep learning and developing ourselves in the Lord and every aspect of life so that we can have great impact on our teens and children.

Our goals

  • To teach the teens and children the sincere word of God and nurture them to develop a passionate relationship with the Lord early in life.
  • To ignite in them a personal discovery of their purpose and calling in Christ Jesus.
  • To raise teens and children who will not only excel academically but will be morally upright and socially responsible.
  • To help them develop skills and talents for service in the household of faith.
  • To encourage them to develop a cordial relationship with parents, peers, younger ones and elders.
  • To encourage teenagers and children to confidently share the gospel with their peers.

Who can Join?

At the Junior Church, we believe there is a place for:

  • A Teenager or Child who wants to have a personal relationship with Jesus.
  • A Teenager or Child who needs mentorship and guardianship.
  • A Teenager or Child who needs a place to belong.
  • A Teenager or Child who needs a place to serve.
  • A Teenager or Child who needs a place to encourage and also to be encouraged.
  • A Teenager or Child who needs a place to grow.
