ABOUT US

Who We Are.

The Good Women of The Redeemed Christian Church of God, Region 21 HQ, Glory House Parish, is one of the five recognised natural group of the Church.

We are sisters, mothers, and indeed the daughters of Abraham fashioned after the similitude of Sarah our Mother. We are beautifully and wonderfully made. We possess the abilities of good and virtuous women. We have been saved and washed in the pool of the Blood of Jesus. Also, we are serving our Lord Jesus with full ofrce commitment, we zre loyal to the calling of God on us, and we do not take God for fun.

“We are God's handiwork, created in Christ Jesus to do good works, which God prepared in advance for us to do. - Ephesians 2:10”

What We Do.

We are concerned with helping one another to be fulfilled. We help to encourage every member of our group to fulfil destiny.
As Destiny moulders, being mothers, we make sure that we have a close and robust relationship with our children and spouses.
Our expectant mothers are thoroughly followed with our prayerss till their safe delivery.

As women, we understand our uniqueness and unique roles in the home, church, and society at large. We treat topics that capture these essences. We minister to the whole, total and complete woman, by tackling her spiritual, physical, emotional, social, and even financial needs.

“Beloved, I pray that you may prosper in all things and be in health, just as your soul prospers” (3 John 2)”

Our Meetings.

We meet on every 2nd Saturday of the Month for Prayers, Skill Acquisitions as well as various lecutres: 7am to 8:30am.

We also meet every 3rd Sunday of the Month. (Immediately after the Worship service).

Our Annual Sisters Conference is slated for February (Yearly).
